# Department of Energy (DOE)

APIs.json 0.19 provider profile for the U.S. Department of Energy.

## Overview

The U.S. Department of Energy provides REST APIs for energy consumption data, renewable energy statistics, fuel prices, electric vehicle infrastructure data, and scientific research publications. Primary API programs include:

- **EIA Open Data API** — Energy time-series data (electricity, natural gas, petroleum, coal, nuclear, renewables)
- **NREL/NLR Developer Network** — Renewable energy, transportation, solar, wind, wave, buildings, and climate APIs
- **OSTI.GOV API** — DOE-funded scientific research records and publications
- **DOE Data Explorer API** — DOE research datasets
