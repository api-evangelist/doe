The Department of Energy applauded the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission's actions to reform interconnection tariffs for data centers and large energy users, aiming to accelerate power delivery while protecting ratepayers. Secretary Wright said meeting demand requires building more energy infrastructure and bringing new power online faster.
